A **mobile-first**, **dark-mode**, and **offline-ready** educational platform that teaches **Gastrointestinal (GI) Pathology + Liver Pathology** exactly as covered in a **pathology residency GI/liver rotation** or standard pathology texts (e.g., Odze, Burt, WHO classifications — conceptual only).

This app must cover:

- Common benign & malignant epithelial lesions  
- Polyp taxonomy (WHO / standard categories)

- Celiac disease pathology

All content is **synthetic**, **non-diagnostic**, **icon-based**, with **session-only quizzes** and **no data tracking**.

# **7. GI/LIVER PATHOLOGY REASONING FRAMEWORK**

1. **Identify site & orientation** (esophagus/stomach/duodenum/colon/liver)  
2. **Assess architecture first**  
3. **Evaluate inflammation pattern**  
4. **Look for chronicity clues**  
5. **Identify epithelial or hepatocellular atypia**  
6. **Use pattern → differential framework**  
7. **Integrate IHC/molecular (conceptual)**  
8. **Create a broad diagnostic category**  
9. **Note pitfalls and mimics**

Common pitfalls:
- Mistaking reactive atypia for dysplasia  
- Misreading serrated architecture  
- Misinterpreting liver ballooning or interface hepatitis  
- Calling HCC without reticulin/architectural context  
- Overcalling IBD without chronicity
